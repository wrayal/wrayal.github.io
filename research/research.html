Research

My research encompasses a variety of techniques for the low-order modelling of various systems related to fluid dynamics. Examples of a few of them are given below. For further details please see the associated papers.

Modelling of thick films

Most low-order models require that films be thin - how do we overcome this limitation?

Evaporation of non-circular drops

How do non-circular drops evaporate, and how does that affect the resultant residue distribution?

Evaporation of a splitting drop

How do nanoparticles behave in an evaporating drop when an electric field is cutting that drop in two?

Suppressing the coffee-ring effect using electric fields

To what extent can we suppress the well-known coffee-ring effect using judiciously chosen electric fields?

High accuracy electrohydrodynamic modelling with inertia

Can we extend our models to higher accuracy, incorporating inertial effects?

Spatially varying electric fields

Can we use electric fields to selectively stabilise flows in certain regions of space?
